BlockForecast runs an open creator program that lets approved users publish prediction markets on any topic and earn a fee on every trade those markets generate. Unlike Polymarket and Kalshi — where every market is curated internally and user creation is not possible — BlockForecast treats market creation as a first-class feature. Once you're approved, you can launch markets in under two minutes and start earning immediately. There are no caps on how many markets you can create, no vesting schedules, and no approval required for each individual market after your initial access is granted.

## How it works

Every time someone places a trade on a market you created, **0.5% of that trade is credited to your on-platform creator balance** in real time. The fee is locked in at market creation — it doesn't change if the platform's protocol fee structure changes later. When you withdraw to your wallet, the platform deducts a 30% withdrawal cut and sends the remaining 70% as USDC on Base. So a market that does $50,000 in lifetime volume generates $250 in gross fees and **$175 net to your wallet**. [Full mechanics →](/creators/earn-fees)

The LSMR automated market maker provides instant liquidity from the moment your market is live, so you don't need to recruit counter-traders. Your job is to write good markets and point an audience at them.

## How BlockForecast compares

Both Polymarket and Kalshi are closed to user-created markets. Every market on those platforms is written and approved by internal teams, which means your topic ideas — however good — can't get listed unless the platform staff decides to create them. BlockForecast is the only major crypto prediction market where the creation pathway is open via application.

## Benefits of creator access

- **0.5% fee on every trade** — credited in real time, withdraw anytime once balance ≥ $10 (30% platform cut at withdrawal; net 70% to your wallet)
- **No limit on markets** — launch as many as you want across any covered topic category
- **Creator dashboard** — per-market volume, earnings history, and trader analytics
- **Creator profile page** — traders can follow your profile and discover your markets
- **Featured creator slots** — high-volume creators get additional platform distribution

<Note>
  Creator access is required before you can publish a market. You do not need to reapply for each new market — one approval covers all your future markets.
</Note>
