## How your data is used

BlockForecast uses the data it collects to:

- **Operate the platform** — execute trades, settle markets, distribute creator fees, and display your portfolio.
- **Improve the product** — analyze usage patterns to identify bugs, improve performance, and prioritize features.
- **Comply with legal obligations** — retain records as required by applicable law, and respond to valid legal requests.
- **Communicate with you** — respond to support requests and, if you've opted in, send product updates.

BlockForecast does not sell your personal data to third parties.

## On-chain data and public visibility

Because BlockForecast is built on Base L2, your wallet address, trade history, and market positions are recorded on a public blockchain. This data is visible to anyone who queries the Base L2 ledger directly — it is outside the scope of any privacy policy. If you want to keep your trading activity private, use a wallet address you haven't publicly associated with your identity.

## Cookies and third-party services

BlockForecast's website may use cookies and third-party analytics tools (such as basic traffic analytics). These are used only for product improvement. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some platform functionality.

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data BlockForecast holds about you. To exercise these rights, contact [support@blockforecast.io](mailto:support@blockforecast.io) with your request.
